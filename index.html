About

I have been working on Deep Learning for quite a while now. I majorly focus on the mathematics and intuitions behind algorithms so that I become capable of doing research in the fields of DL. I have great exposure in other fields also like Robotics Perception, Electronics, Natural Language Processing and CAD designing. I mainly work with images and signals and wish to contribute more. I have great team spirit and have completed huge projects in a short time under my leadership. I have also mentored few projects which has procured international research papers and patents.
